### How Railway's Hobby Plan Actually Works

**Based on Railway's official pricing page:**

### What You Pay
- **$5 upfront** to activate the Hobby plan (one-time activation fee)
- **$5/month minimum** (includes $5 of usage)
- **Additional charges** if you exceed $5 usage in a month

### What You Get
- **$5 of usage included** every month (after activation)
- 8 GB RAM / 8 vCPU per service
- Single developer workspace
- Community Support
- 7-day log history
- Global regions

### What You Need
- ‚ö†Ô∏è **Credit card required** (mandatory)
- **$5 upfront payment** to activate the plan
- Railway will charge you for usage above $5 at the end of each billing cycle

### Example Usage

**Activation:**
- You pay: **$5 upfront** to activate Hobby plan
- You get: $5 of usage included for the first month

**Month 1:**
- You use $3 worth of resources
- Railway charges: **$0 additional** (you're within included $5)
- Total cost this month: $5 (activation) + $0 = **$5**

**Month 2:**
- Railway includes $5 of usage (monthly)
- You use $4 worth of resources
- Railway charges: **$5** (monthly minimum) + $0 = **$5**
- Total cost: **$5**

**Month 3:**
- Railway includes $5 of usage (monthly)
- You use $7 worth of resources
- Railway charges: **$5** (monthly minimum) + $2 (excess) = **$7**
- Total cost: **$7**
